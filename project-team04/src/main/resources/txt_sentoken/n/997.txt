In 2004, imatinib was approved for use as a first-line treatment for GIST after phase II and III clinical trials yielded overall response rates of approximately 50%, a 1-year survival rate greater than 80%, and a 2-year survival rate around 70% (Joensuu et al, 2001; Dagher et al, 2002; Demetri et al, 2002, 2005; Verweij et al, 2004).
